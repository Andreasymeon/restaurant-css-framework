Wild Feast

We do things as they have always been done. Fires are our main cuisine method. We are always looking out to find forgotten ways to cook or prepare our food and bring that back to life in our modern lives. It is warm and primitive. It is an experience almost like a show you can watch while drinking a hot drink around the fire. Our palette of inspirations comes from all around the world.

We follow seasonal variations and our existing traditions. During the summer and autumn, we harvest what grows on our land as it reaches the peak of ripeness, and prepare it using methods we have rediscovered from rich traditions, or that we have created through our own research to maintain the highest quality of the end product. We dry, salt, jelly, pickle and bottle. The hunting season starts after the harvest and is an important time, when we take advantage of the exceptional bounty with which the mountains provide us. By the time spring and summer return, the cupboard is bare and the cycle begins again.
