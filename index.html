About

We do things as they have always been done. Fires are our main cuisine method. We are always looking out to find forgotten ways to cook or prepare our food and bring that back to life in our modern lives.

We follow seasonal variations and our existing traditions. During the summer and autumn, we harvest what grows on our land as it reaches the peak of ripeness, and prepare it using methods we have rediscovered from rich traditions, or that we have created through our own research to maintain the highest quality of the end product.

We dry, salt, jelly, pickle and bottle. The hunting season starts after the harvest and is an important time, when we take advantage of the exceptional bounty with which the mountains provide us. By the time spring and summer return, the cupboard is bare and the cycle begins again.

Menu

  • Seafood Season

    At its absolute best during the cold, winter months.
    Each serving on the menu will include some element from the ocean.
    Please keep this in mind when booking a table.

    70€

  • Vegetable Season

    From early summer to early fall. This menu is perfect for
    vegetarians and vegans and will make full use of our garden.
    Our foragers will also be working overtime to maximize the time of the year
    when the plant kingdom reigns supreme.

    50€

  • Forest Season

    From early fall to January. This is the only period of the year
    when meat will play a starring role in our dishes.
    During this season, we’ll pay homage to the animals and things that grow from the forest:
    berries, mushrooms, nuts, and wild plants, along with the bounty of the harvest season.

    60€
